Radio-Canada interview with Dr. Alexa Pichet Binette

October 2025

A Blood Test to Detect Alzheimer’s Disease Dr. Alexa Pichet Binette appeared on the radio show “Les années lumière” on Radio-Canada/CBC to discuss the latest advances in blood tests for the early detection of Alzheimer's disease. An insightful conversation about how scientific progress is transforming the way we diagnose Alzheimer's disease.

New master's student in the laboratory

September 2025

We are pleased to welcome Sebastian Rodriguez, who joined our laboratory in September to complete a master's degree in biomedical engineering at the Université de Montreal. With his experience in deep learning, MRI analysis, and computational neuroscience, his project will involve developing predictive models to facilitate the diagnosis and prognosis of Alzheimer's disease. We are delighted to welcome him to our team!

New funding – Digital Health Network

September 2025

The project, developed in collaboration with Dr. Julien Cohen-Adad (Functional Neuroimaging Unit, UNF), aims to create a personalized digital report on neurodegeneration by integrating neuroimaging and plasma biomarkers into the clinical diagnosis of neurocognitive disorders. The co-investigators are Drs. Sylvia Villeneuve, Lune Bellec, and Thomas Tannou.
